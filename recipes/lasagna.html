Basic Ass Lasagna!

Description

Alright, sugar, this lasagna? It’s basic, plain and simple, like real basic.
You boil your noodles, throw on some jarred sauce, cook up some ground beef,
don’t even try to season it like you know what you’re doin, and dump some store-bought shredded mozzarella on top.
Pop it in the oven and boom, dinner’s done. Ain’t no real recipe here, just a quick fix for when you ain't got time or energy.
It’ll fill you up, but don’t come here expectin' no family heirloom or somethin' special, ‘cause this ain’t it.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt & pepper to taste

How to make the dang thang!

  1. Cook the noodles : Boil lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce : In a pan, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat, then stir in marinara sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix the ricotta filling : In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Assemble the lasagna : In a baking dish, layer as follows:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Add a layer of noodles.
    • Spread some ricotta mixture on top.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese.
    • Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with sauce and a final sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  5. Bake : Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  6. Rest & Serve : Let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
